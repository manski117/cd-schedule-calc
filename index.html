▲ Camp Day-Off Scheduler

Builds a season of staff days off that respect your in-camp floor, contracts, and required dates — entirely in your browser.

1Camp basics

The minimum % is the single most important rule: after scatter day, the calculator never lets in-camp head-count drop below it in any half-day. The season runs May 1 – Aug 20; before scatter, staff are at training (working days, no in-camp floor yet), and days off there happen only as required must-haves.
